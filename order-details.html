❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I select a table? ▼
Click on any available table number from the categories above. Selected tables will be highlighted in yellow.
What is cover charge? ▼
Cover charge includes table service, ambiance, and is adjustable against your food bill. Each category has different cover charges.
Can I change my table? ▼
Yes, you can change your table selection anytime before proceeding to the menu. Simply click on a different available table.
What if my table is booked? ▼
Booked tables are marked with a cross (×) and cannot be selected. Please choose from available tables only.
