Frequently Asked Questions
How does table selection work? ▼
Simply click any available table. You'll be automatically redirected to the menu to complete your order.
What is cover charge? ▼
Cover charge includes table service and is fully adjustable against your final food bill.
Can I change my selection? ▼
Yes, you can change tables anytime before placing your order by clicking a different table.
What do grey tables mean? ▼
Greyed out tables are currently booked. Please select from the highlighted available tables.
